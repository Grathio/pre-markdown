Rendering Options

A Side-by-side comparison of default conversion and conversion with options set. The bodies of the Markdown are the same, only the flags are different.

View source on the page to see the <pre-markdown> tag in action below.

Default:

- Rendering of Inline HTML. - Formatting of standard "quotes". - Conversion of strings like (c) and (r) into entities. - Conversion of link-like things into links: - URLS: https://some.link - Email: some@email.ad Conversion of newlines within paragraphs to `
`s.

Uses the simple tag: <pre-markdown>>

With Options:

- Rendering of Inline HTML. - Formatting of standard "quotes". - Conversion of strings like (c) and (r) into entities. - Conversion of link-like things into links: - URLS: https://some.link - Email: some@email.ad Conversion of newlines within paragraphs to `
`s.

Uses the tag: <pre-markdown html="true" linkify="true" typographer="true" breaks="true">
